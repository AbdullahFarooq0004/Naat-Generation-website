Introducing Naat Generator

 

Naat Generation offers instant access to Naat Sharifs in multiple languages – Urdu, Sindhi, English, and Pashto. Simply input keywords and select your preferred language to generate a unique Naat Sharif tailored to your desires.

About Us
The intersection of poetry and technology. Modern AI allows for "poetry generation," where algorithms analyze existing poems and create new ones. This project specifically focuses on Naat, a form of Islamic poetry praising Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ). By using AI and a vast dataset of Naats, the project aims to create new Naats in Urdu, Sindhi, and Punjabi, keeping the tradition relevant and accessible.
